## Reading the table

- **Coverage % near 100** → that zone type was effectively always available; respect rate from Phase 1 reflects calibration, not availability.
- **Coverage % low (<20%)** → that zone type was rarely or never present; Phase 1 respect rate cannot be interpreted, treat as data-quality flag, not edge evidence.
- **Last created stale** (e.g. weeks old) → producer for that pattern type is not running on cadence. Operational issue, not architectural.
- **Zones high but coverage % low** → many short-validity zones (e.g. PDH/PDL valid one day each); arithmetic of zones × validity, not a bug.
